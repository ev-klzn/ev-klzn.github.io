My family

Until February 24, 2022, we, like all citizens of our country, a normal life. We played in the park, ride a bike, made plans for the future and just enjoyed life. After the Russian attack on Ukraine, my family was forced to leave for another country.

This day has come.

The day that destroyed the lives of millions of Ukrainians. Many have left their homes to which they will never return. Now my wife and my son are safe. Many countries responded to help the Ukrainians. One of them is Ireland.

Ireland is beautiful.

Now they live near the city of Ennis.

My son with friends from Ukraine went to school. They really like to study at the new school.

Yesterday they went to Dublin to the zoo.
